Civilizational evolution is modeled as a spectral transition sequence:

UH → US → UQ → UT

Each transition is mediated by a functor Ti: Ui → Ui+1, preserving μ-consistency and logical structure.

The moduli space of modal civilizations allows PAC–μ to represent, simulate, and evolve intelligent structures categorically, topologically, and spectrally.
